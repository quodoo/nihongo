# KHUNG CHUONG TRINH PHAN DONG TU (N5 - N3)

## 1. Muc tieu tong the

Tai lieu dong tu huong den 3 muc tieu:

- Nhin ra nhanh dong tu thuoc nhom nao va chia duoc cac the co ban.
- Hieu dung y nghia ngu phap cua tung the trong giao tiep va de thi JLPT.
- Dung duoc dong tu trong ngu canh thuc te: hoi dap, viet cau, doc hieu.

Phan nay duoc thiet ke cho N5 -> N3, theo nguyen tac:

**Hoc theo truc "Nghia -> Cong thuc -> Ngu canh -> Loi sai"**

---

## 2. Dau ra sau khi hoc xong

Nguoi hoc co the:

- Chia dung cac the dong tu quan trong: masu, te, nai, ta, jisho.
- Dung duoc cac mau nang cao den N3: ukemi, shieki, shieki-ukemi, kanou, ishikei, meireikei, kinshikei.
- Ket hop dong tu voi tro tu dung theo tinh huong.
- Giai bai doc, bai dien cau, bai sua loi lien quan den dong tu.

---

## 3. Cau truc tong the cua tai lieu

Tai lieu chia lam 4 phan lon:

1. Nen tang chia dong tu (N5)
2. Mo rong cong nang va ket hop mau (N4)
3. The nang cao va sac thai (N3)
4. Tong on va luyen de mini-test

---

## 4. Khung chuong de xuat

### PHAN A - NEN TANG (N5)

1. Chuong 1: Nhan dien 3 nhom dong tu va quy tac chia co ban
2. Chuong 2: The masu va jisho (lich su vs than mat)
3. Chuong 3: The te va cac cach dung cot loi
4. Chuong 4: The nai va dien dat phu dinh
5. Chuong 5: The ta (qua khu) va kinh nghiem co ban

### PHAN B - MO RONG (N4)

1. Chuong 6: The kha nang (kanou)
2. Chuong 7: The y dinh / du dinh (ishikei + tsumori)
3. Chuong 8: The cam doan / suy doan (darou, deshou, hazu)
4. Chuong 9: The sai khien (shieki) co ban
5. Chuong 10: Ket hop dong tu voi mau yeu cau, cho phep, cam doan

### PHAN C - NANG CAO (N3)

1. Chuong 11: The bi dong (ukemi) va cach nhin hanh dong
2. Chuong 12: Sai khien bi dong (shieki-ukemi)
3. Chuong 13: Menh lenh, cam doan, khuyen ngung (meirei, kinshi, chuui)
4. Chuong 14: Sac thai noi - viet: rut gon, lich su, trang trong
5. Chuong 15: Cum dong tu de nham (ageru/kureru/morau, shimau, oku, miru)

### PHAN D - TONG ON

1. Chuong 16: Ban do tong hop cac the dong tu
2. Chuong 17: 100 cau mini-test tong hop dong tu
3. Chuong 18: Chua loi chi tiet theo nhom sai

---

## 5. Mau trinh bay cho moi chuong

Moi chuong giu 1 format co dinh (dong bo voi phan tro tu):

1. Muc tieu bai hoc
2. Kien thuc cot loi
3. Bang chia nhanh / cong thuc
4. Vi du phan tich
5. Loi thuong gap
6. Bai tap luyen tap
7. Dap an dua ve phu luc cuoi sach

Goi y them cho phan dong tu:

- Them muc "Mau de thi JLPT" (1-2 dang cau thuong gap).
- Them muc "So sanh de nham" (vi du: nai de wa ikenai vs nakereba naranai).

---

## 6. Lo trinh bien soan (de lam viec tiep)

De tranh qua tai, nen bien soan theo 3 dot:

1. Dot 1: Hoan thanh PHAN A (5 chuong) + mini-test 30 cau.
2. Dot 2: Hoan thanh PHAN B (5 chuong) + mini-test 30 cau.
3. Dot 3: Hoan thanh PHAN C, D + mini-test 40 cau va dap an tong.

---

## 7. Tieu chi chat luong

- Moi the dong tu phai co: cong thuc + it nhat 5 vi du + 5 loi sai dien hinh.
- Bai tap phai co du 3 muc: nhan dien, dien cau, viet lai cau.
- Noi dung bám JLPT N5-N3, uu tien tinh ung dung trong giao tiep.

---

## 8. Buoc tiep theo de trien khai

Neu ban dong y khung nay, buoc tiep theo nen la:

1. Chot danh sach chuong cuoi cung (giu 18 chuong hoac rut gon).
2. Bat dau viet chi tiet Chuong 1 theo dung format da chot.
3. Sau moi 2 chuong se tao mini-review de kiem tra do mach lac.
